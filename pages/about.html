About This Project

The Media Literacy Quiz was created for the UNESCO Hackathon to help raise awareness about misinformation, fake news, and how to identify trustworthy sources.

Our goal is to empower people, especially students and young audiences, to think critically about the information they consume online.

What You’ll Learn

  • How to recognize fake news headlines
  • Spotting bias in articles
  • Understanding reliable sources
  • Practicing safe online sharing

Team

This project was built by beginners in programming as part of the UNESCO Hackathon. We believe media literacy is a key skill for today’s digital world.
